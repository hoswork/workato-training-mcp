# Workato Connector Catalog

Reference for connector descriptions and available triggers/actions. Use this file when:
- Analyzing what a customer's active connectors do (Step 2, Recipe Deep Dive)
- Validating that a proposed scenario uses the connector in its correct business role (Step 6, Criterion 1)
- Writing accurate trigger/action names in scenario descriptions and trainer artifacts

**How to use:** Look up a connector by name (file is alphabetical). Check the description for business role and category. Check Triggers & Actions to confirm the specific integration is possible.
